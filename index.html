OUR PHILOSOPHY

At Mint, a waterfront cafe located in Malta, we strive to bring you the best in seasonal fresh cuisine. We only rely on the best quality produce, delivered daily and where possible try to sustain our produce locally and organically.

OUR TEAM

Service is of paramount importance to us at Mint. Our cafe manager, Kerry has been working in the hospitality industry for many years and together with the rest of our team strive to ensure our customer service is the best on the island.

WHAT MAKES US DIFFERENT

Our passion, drive and our desire to create new exciting dishes so even our regular customer can experience something new on a daily basis.

NEW OPENING HOURS 2018

Mint is now open from Monday to Friday from 8am to 4pm. We will be closed on Saturday & Sunday. Now here’s a reason to start loving Mondays!

TAKE US HOME

Everything on display can be prepared for you to take away. If you enjoyed our homemade dressings on your salad or one of our relishes we can jar them up for you to take home. Should you wish to attempt one of our recipes you’ll find an array of different spices and condiments in our retail section. Some of which are Zaatar, Spanish smoked paprika, Israeli cous cous and Ras al hanut.

OUR CAFÉ

The concept behind Mint is derived from the typical New Zealand style café. Beautifully displayed cabinets offering tempting food all in a relaxed atmosphere of counter order, sit down service.

CAPUCHINO

One of the most popular coffee variations nowadays.

ESPRESSO

This famous Italian coffee drink has a delicate taste and aroma.

AMERICANO

An espresso-based drink designed to resemble coffee in a cup.

OUR FOOD

Having travelled the world experiencing many different food cultures has enabled us to bring together diversity in our food, we enjoy attempting new flavour combinations whilst also relying on old favourites.

OUR TREATS

At Mint we believe the best cakes are made from scratch. We make all our pastry, cookies, slices and loaves in the Min kitchen. This way we control and maintain the high standards of baking we’re renowned for. All our whole cakes are made to order so we require 48hrs notice.
